# 🔴 Pokémon Restock Cheat Sheet — Sandra

**Watching for:** Ascended Heroes Elite Trainer Boxes (Target + Walmart, Waterbury)

## When your phone buzzes (Telegram)
1. Open the alert. Tap **BUY NOW**.
2. Check out as FAST as you can. Don't overthink it — grab it, we sort it out later.
3. If it's a **pickup** alert, place the pickup order, then swing by the store.

*Turn Telegram notifications for the "Pokémon Alerts" group to LOUD / priority so
you don't miss a daytime drop.*

---

## Best times to physically check the stores
*(all times are our local time)*

### 🎯 Target (300 Chase Ave)
- **Brand-new sets:** drop overnight, around **3 AM** (rare you'll catch these live —
  the bot is better for these).
- **Restocks of stuff already out:** **3 PM – 6 PM**, and **Friday is the big day**
  (Monday/Tuesday afternoons are next best).

### 🛒 Walmart (910 Wolcott St)
- **Online:** **Wednesday & Thursday mornings, 6 AM – 10 AM.**
- **In store:** trucks come overnight **Tuesday/Wednesday**; workers put boxes out
  **around 7 AM – 10 AM.** Card aisle + toy section.

---

## Two things that beat the scalpers
1. **Ask a Walmart stocker:** "What morning does your Pokémon card vendor come?"
   That one answer tells us exactly when to show up. Vendors usually come Tue–Thu.
2. **On Walmart.com, filter to "Sold & shipped by Walmart"** — that skips the
   marketplace scalpers reselling at markup.

## Have this ready for 1-tap checkout
- Saved payment + shipping address in the Target app AND Walmart app.
- Logged in on your phone ahead of time.

*Keep it on the fridge. When in doubt: buy first, ask questions later. 💪*
